Bad Ordering Example

The head elements above are out of order and will be flagged by the require-order rule.

Optimal Order (from capo.js):

  1. META (charset, viewport, CSP) - weight 10

  4. ASYNC_SCRIPT - weight 7
  5. IMPORT_STYLES - weight 6
  6. SYNC_SCRIPT - weight 5
  7. SYNC_STYLES - weight 4
  8. PRELOAD - weight 3
  9. DEFER_SCRIPT - weight 2
  10. PREFETCH/PRERENDER - weight 1
